# Maren's Guard

## Purpose

Maren's Guard keeps the Ashen Citadel obedient while Lord Maren waits for the last Voss heir to reveal herself. They enforce curfew, seize ember relics, and make public examples of suspected loyalists.

## Power Base

The Guard controls the city gates, ration lines, ember-worker permits, and prison cells beneath the High Keep. Their authority rests on fear, cold streets, and Maren's claim that only strict rule can preserve the Northern Reach.

## Members

- **Lord Maren** - ruler of the citadel and final authority over the Guard
- **Gate captains** - patrol the city tiers and mountain roads
- **Sanctioned ember-workers** - controlled practitioners used for heat, interrogation, and intimidation

## Conflicts

The Guard can hold the city but cannot restore its warmth. Their raids push citizens toward quiet resistance and make Sera's return more dangerous for everyone who might shelter her.
