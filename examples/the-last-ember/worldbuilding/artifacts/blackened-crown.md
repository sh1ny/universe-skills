# Blackened Crown

## Description

The Blackened Crown is a narrow circlet of dark ember-steel, scorched along one side from the night Lord Maren seized the throne. Its inner rim still bears the Voss oath in silver script.

## Function

The crown has no working magic left, but it remains the public symbol of rule over the Northern Reach. Wearing it lets Maren claim continuity with the dynasty he destroyed.

## History

Queen Elara wore the crown during winter rites at the Ember Well. Maren took it after the coup and refused to repair the scorch marks, turning the damage into a warning.

## Current State

The crown rests in the High Keep audience chamber. Sera sees it as stolen inheritance; Maren uses it as proof that survival matters more than bloodline.
