# Harbor Council

## Purpose

The Harbor Council keeps Port Kestrel powered, moored, rationed, and officially calm. Its public purpose is disaster coordination. Its private purpose, under Councillor Venn, is keeping the Blackout Night record sealed.

## Power Base

Council power comes from mooring rights, battery allocation, salvage permits, storm warnings, and access to the sealed maintenance archives. Residents who depend on grid access cannot openly oppose the Council without risking their docks.

## Members

- **Councillor Ilya Venn** - public administrator and private custodian of the Afterimage Archive
- **Harbor clerks** - control permits, records, and ration ledgers
- **Storm-grid officers** - enforce emergency orders during surge season

## Conflicts

The Council needs Mara Quill quiet because her search for Theo's evidence threatens the official Blackout Night story. Dock Six resents the Council's authority but still depends on its storm-grid repairs.
