# Theo Quill

## Appearance

Theo had Mara's dark eyes but none of her stillness. He was narrow-shouldered, quick-handed, and always inked with grease from panels he had opened without permission. His Harbor Authority badge photo showed him smiling too broadly, as if he had already spotted the joke in the machine.

## Personality & Traits

Restless, funny, and too clever to leave a broken system alone. Theo believed infrastructure had a moral life: if a pump failed, someone had ignored it; if a warning light stayed dark, someone had chosen not to see. He joked when nervous and kept second copies of everything.

**Strengths:** Brilliant systems thinker, brave under technical pressure, loyal to Dock Six
**Flaws:** Secretive, underestimated political danger, trusted evidence more than people

## Backstory

Theo joined the Harbor Authority at twenty-three and quickly became one of the engineers responsible for Port Kestrel's storm grid. He discovered that Councillor Venn had repurposed obsolete memory therapy hardware into an illegal archive beneath Bellwether Reef. Theo copied enough data to prove it, then died during the Blackout Night before he could get the evidence to Mara.

The official report blamed him for opening the wrong breakers during the storm. Mara never believed it, but Theo had hidden his investigation so well that disbelief was all she had.

## Motivations & Goals

**Primary:** Expose the Afterimage Archive before Venn can erase it
**Secondary:** Keep Mara away from the people who killed him
**Internal:** Believe his town can survive the truth
**Fear:** That the archive's victims will be hurt twice: once by theft, once by exposure

## Voice & Speech Patterns

Fast, joking, precise when explaining machinery. Uses nicknames for systems he dislikes. In recordings, he talks to Mara as if she is about to interrupt him.

> "Do not give me that face. Yes, I made three backups. No, you do not get to ask where yet."

> "If I am wrong, you can call me dramatic. If I am dead, please skip that part."

## Character Arc

**Starting state:** Known publicly as the man who caused the Blackout Night
**Midpoint:** His recording reframes him as a witness, not a saboteur
**End state:** His choices force Mara to balance truth with consent
